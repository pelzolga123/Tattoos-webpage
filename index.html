ABOUT US

We are a small family owned business that was established
in Sweden back in 2006. The company, founded by Svetlyo,
later on, expanded and opened its primary location in the heart
of the tattoo industry - San Francisco, California.

Our shop is client-centered, and our mission is to make
everyone feel great in their skin. From the first day, we opened
our doors in 2010, we've strived for excellence in creating truly
unique custom tattoos for you.
